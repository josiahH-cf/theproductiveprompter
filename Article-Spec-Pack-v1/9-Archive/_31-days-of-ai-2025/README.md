This folder contains workflow artifacts created during the **31 Days of AI (Dec 2025)** campaign.

Scope
- This archive is **only** for Article-Spec-Pack workflow inputs/outputs (briefs, source drafts, packaged article folders, and campaign planning files).

Contents
